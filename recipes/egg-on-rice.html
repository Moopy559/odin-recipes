Egg On Rice

An easy breakfast inspired by Korean cooking staples

Egg, rice and an assortment of sauces make this a quick and easy meal to make first thing in the morning. Not to mention extremely tasty as well!

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Roasted Sesame Seeds
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Cook 1 cup of rice with water on the stove
  2. In a separate frying pan, add vegetable oil and 2 eggs, cook on low for 3 minutes
  3. Remove rice and eggs from heat and place in bowl
  4. Sprinkle a drizzle of soy sauce, sesame oil and roasted sesame seeds - enjoy!
