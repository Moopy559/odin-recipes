Green Curry

A famous Thai dish known for it's fragrance and iconic green colour

It has a less “in your face” flavour compared to Thai Red Curry but it's more fragrant with chilli than Thai Yellow Curry. Creamy, sweet, and salty. But all too often, Thai takeout places make it far too sweet!

Ingredients

  • Green Curry Paste
  • Coconut Milk
  • Lime Leaves
  • Fish Sauce
  • Raw Sugar
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Carrot
  • Zucchini
  • Red Capsicum
  • Thai basil

Steps

  1. Add oil and curry paste to wok, cook on high for 2 minutes
  2. Add coconut milk and reduce until thickened
  3. Add raw sugar and fish sauce to taste
  4. Add diced vegetables and cook until softened
  5. Add diced chicken thighs, cook until no longer pink
  6. Add thai basil and lime leaves, cook for 1 minute
  7. remove from heat and serve with rice
