Nachos

Oven baked chips served with sour cream, salsa and cheese.

Requiring only one tray, this is the perfect dish for easy clean ups. Simply throw it in the oven after prepping the ingredients and you are done!

Ingredients

  • Corn Chips
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees, fan forced
  2. Line oven tray with foil and coat with corn chips
  3. Coat corn chips with a jar of salsa and a bag of shredded cheese
  4. Place in oven for 15 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and top with a dollop of sour cream - enjoy!
